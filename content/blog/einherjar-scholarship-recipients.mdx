Asgard University is proud to announce the **2025 Einherjar Scholarship** recipients—forty-two mortals whose courage, intellect, and character embody the spirit of the golden halls.

## Selection Criteria

Recipients were chosen from over 4,000 applicants based on:

- Academic excellence across all six realms
- Leadership in community and extracurricular pursuits
- Personal essays demonstrating resilience and vision

## A Tradition of Excellence

The Einherjar Scholarship, established in 1923, has supported more than 1,200 scholars who have gone on to lead in government, science, arts, and commerce. This year's cohort represents 28 nations and every academic realm.

Congratulations to all recipients. The full list is available in the Valhalla Registrar's office and on the student portal.
