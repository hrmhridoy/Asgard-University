The **2025 Aurora Symposium** concluded with record-breaking attendance as scholars, alumni, and industry partners gathered beneath the Golden Arch to celebrate innovation across the six realms.

## Highlights

- **Best in Show**: Alfheim's quantum entanglement communication prototype
- **People's Choice**: Vanaheim's immersive Norse saga VR experience
- **Emerging Scholar Award**: First-year student Loki Svensson, Nidavellir Engineering

## Looking Ahead

Planning for the 2026 symposium begins immediately. Faculty are encouraged to nominate student presenters by December 1.
