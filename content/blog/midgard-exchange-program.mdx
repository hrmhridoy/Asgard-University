The **Midgard Exchange Program** has officially launched, welcoming eighty scholars from partner universities across Europe, Asia, Africa, and the Americas for a full semester of study at Asgard University.

## Program Highlights

Exchange students will:

- Enroll in courses across any of the six academic realms
- Participate in the Aurora Welcome cultural immersion week
- Collaborate on the annual Bifrost Symposium research project

## Building Global Bridges

Dean of Midgard Studies, Professor Helga Earthsong, remarked: "Our mission is to prepare leaders who understand that wisdom knows no borders. These scholars bring perspectives that enrich every hall."

Applications for the spring 2026 exchange cycle open September 1. Visit the Global Affairs office or contact midgard@asgard.edu for details.
